About

I am a Ph.D. student in Operations Research at the School of Industrial Engineering, Purdue University, advised by Prof. Susan R. Hunter. I was admitted to the joint M.S. program in Statistics and Computer Science at Purdue University in Fall 2024, and joined the Ph.D. program in the School of Industrial Engineering as a dual-degree student in Fall 2025. I received my undergraduate degree in Summer 2024 from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, where I majored in Economic Statistics with a minor in Computer Science.

My research focuses on stochastic set-valued optimization, multi-stage multi-objective decision-making, probability theory and statistical simulation. I am also interested in large-scale optimization, data science, machine learning and reinforcement learning.
